History

In her 126 year long history, Juventus has won countless trophies. A list of the major ones includes:

Domestic

  • Italian Championship: 34 Times
  • Coppa Italia 13 Times
  • Supercoppa Italian 7 Times

International

  • Champions League: 2 Times
  • UEFA Cup 3 Times
  • UEFA Cup Winners' Cup 1 Time
  • UEFA Super Cup 2 Times
  • Intercontinental Cup 2 Times
